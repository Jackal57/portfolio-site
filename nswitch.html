Nintendo Switch 3D Model

This project is a fully modeled and functioning render of a Nintendo Switch with a custom designed wooden game cabinet and an accurate render of a Samsung Smart TV for scale. The models were designed in Autodesk Fusion 360 as the final grade of a 3D modeling class I took in undergrad. Just like a real Switch, the Switch render has a screen that detaches from the base, as well as detachable joycons. All the Switch buttons on the render are fully functional and the joysticks have a full range of movement. The game cabinet has two movable drawers to store games, as well as a sliding shelf upon which the Switch is placed.
